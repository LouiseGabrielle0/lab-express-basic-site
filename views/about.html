Rick Grimes

Leader. Father. Hero. Killer.

Younger Life

Rick grew up in Georgia, somewhere near Atlanta. As a child, he shared a close relationship with his father whom he described as a 'profound' man who instilled a strong moral code onto him. His mother often told him the story of; The Stone in The Road, which encouraged him to never give up in the face of adversity. He also became inspired by the old tales of his grandfather, whom served during the Second World War.
